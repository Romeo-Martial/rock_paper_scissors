Rock • Paper • Scissors

The game is played between you and the computer. Each player simultaneously chooses one of three options: rock, paper, or scissors. The winner is determined by the rules:

  • 🪨 Rock crushes Scissors
  • 📄 Scissors cuts Paper
  • ✂️ Paper covers Rock

First to reach the target score wins!
